Goa, India


Goa, the beach paradise of India, is known for its stunning coastline, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage. It offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure, making it a must-visit destination.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Baga Beach - Famous for its lively shacks, water sports, and nightlife.
  2. Dudhsagar Waterfalls - A spectacular four-tiered waterfall amidst lush greenery.
  3. Fort Aguada - A historic fort with stunning views of the Arabian Sea.
  4. Basilica of Bom Jesus - A UNESCO World Heritage site housing the remains of St. Francis Xavier.
  5. Anjuna Flea Market - A vibrant market offering unique handicrafts and souvenirs.
